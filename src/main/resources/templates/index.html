Who We Are

#EndGasFlaring is an initiative formed to create awareness and also draw the attention of the World to plight of the people in the Niger Delta region of Nigeria. This initiative's sole purpose is to end the barbaric and archaic practice of gas flaring which has decimated the region's environment and as a consequence, eliminated the agricultural advantage of a once fertile land.

We Care

We're from these communities and we have put ourselves in their shoes and we deeply care about the environment our children play, eat, attend school and grow up.

We Believe

We believe that this crime should not continue. We believe that we deserve clean air and water. We believe that we are on the right side of history and that future generations will judge us based on our accomplishments and not what we did not do.

We're Determined

Our determination stems from the momentum we have behind us. The world is aware of the effects of CO2 in the environment as well as Climate Change. The world is moving towards cleaner technologies and we are in the forefront of innovation.

What We Do

Community Engagement

We see the affected communities as the main stakeholders in our endeavor and our partnership with them is invaluable as we build smart micro grids to alleviate the scourge of gas flaring.

Gas Flare Mitigation

The time to end gas flaring is long overdue, our technology turns the flames off and replaces it with affordable electricity and cooking gas

Agricultural Investment

The adverse effects of gas flaring on the soil is indescribable but we still believe that the way forward is to make ample investments in agriculture by introducing world class best practices to achieve a bountiful harvest

Environmental Clean-up

According to UNEP, it will take close to 30 years to reverse the damage that has been done. We accept the challenge.

Modular Gas Refinement

Our modular systems will refine flare gas on site and provide cooking gas as well as fuels for our power generation turbines.

Industrial Stimulation

The fuel captured will stimulate local industries since we will be providing cheap energy for their operations.
